Monthly 
	- Date
		- Repeats on <Dates>[] every n months
	- Day of Week
		- Repeats on the <First> <Monday> every n months

Yearly 
	- Date
		- Repeats on <Month>[] every n years
	- Day
		- Repeats on the <First> <Monday> of <Month>[] every n years

Weekly
	- Date
		- Repeats on <Weekday>[] every n weeks

Daily 
	- Repeats everyday (if every == 1 else Repeats every n days)
